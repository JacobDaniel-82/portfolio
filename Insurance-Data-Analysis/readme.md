# Insurance Data Analysis (Power BI)

## Overview
This project analyzes insurance business data for **PRISM Insurance Pvt. Ltd.** (simulated organization) using Power BI. The focus is on building clear, business-oriented dashboards and extracting customer sentiment insights from feedback data.

The project combines operational analysis with sentiment analysis to provide a holistic view of business performance and customer experience.

---

## Problem Statement
Insurance companies generate large volumes of operational and customer feedback data.  
This project aims to analyze insurance performance metrics while also understanding customer sentiment to identify areas of strength and improvement.

---

## Dataset
- Organization: PRISM Insurance Pvt. Ltd. (simulated)
- Data type:
  - Policy and customer details
  - Business performance metrics
  - Customer feedback text
- Usage: Analytics and visualization

---

## Tech Stack
- Power BI
- Power Query Editor (PQE)
- DAX
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Sentiment Analysis (Rule-based)

---

## Dashboard Structure
### Page 1: Business Overview
- Key insurance KPIs
- Overall performance summary
- High-level business insights

### Page 2: Detailed Records
- Tabular view of customer and policy-level data
- Drill-down for detailed analysis

### Page 3: Customer Sentiment Analysis
- Sentiment classification of customer feedback
- Categories:
  - Excellent
  - Good
  - Needs Improvement
- Sentiment score for each customer feedback
- Distribution of sentiment across customers

---

## Key Insights
- Overview of insurance business performance
- Identification of customer satisfaction levels
- Distribution of positive vs negative feedback
- Areas requiring service improvement based on sentiment trends

---

## Outputs
- Interactive Power BI dashboard
- Business performance and sentiment insights
- Customer-level sentiment scoring
